# About

We make synth-driven music shaped by magic realism: songs where everyday life coexists with hidden correspondences between matter, language, place, memory, and feeling. What seems mundane at first gradually reveals symbolic depth, without ever ceasing to be real.

Our music begins in lived reality — cities, travel, technologies, names, relationships, and chance encounters — and lets these things open into poetry, symbol, and quiet transcendence. A chemical term can become emotional logic. A translated expression can become a love song. A real place can become both itself and a symbolic landscape.

Musically, we live near synthpop, post-punk, new wave, and pop rock, but our deeper territory lies beyond genre: in the hidden resonance of ordinary life.

Our lyrics often draw on cultural and scientific references, puns, and layered meanings. But this density is not there for its own sake: it is a way to compress more into each song, so that stories shaped like legends, romances, and journeys can unfold in unusual settings without wasting words.
